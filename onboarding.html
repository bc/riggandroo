Utility Setup for Your New Home

Welcome to your new home! To help you get settled, we've compiled a list of essential utilities for the Denver area.

Denver Water: Water & Sewer

Denver Water Website
Call Denver Water Customer Care (303) 893-2444 [7:30am-5:30pm M-F] 0-2 days after you have moved in to set up your water. $30-55 is the expected utility bill for Water+Sewer

You will need to verify the name of the owner: Brian Cohn

Internet

Xfinity offers internet services in this region. The fastest way to get set up is to visit an Xfinity Store during business hours, set up an account, and they'll hand you a router. Coax port is in the kitchen for all units. (directions to the nearest Xfinity Store to the property (they validate parking)). Xfinity Website

Gas and Power

Xcel Energy is responsible for gas and power services. Xcel Energy Website
